About Me

Hi, I'm Felipe from Brazil, I'm a software developer for 12 years and entrepreneur since ever, I've already worked on various projects, from small and innovative startups to legacies softwares in multinational companies and government. I have work experience as a Senior Developer in several places in Europe and in Australia and my own awarded startup accelered in Chile.

I've started my career with PHP and MySql, I went through many years developing in .NET Framework and C#, I've worked with Java abroad and now I am back and I returned to my favourite language which I learned at the university 12 years ago how develop systems, when it just was considered a joke: JavaScript.

I created this blog intenting to enlarge my skils and help my friends to learn programming and the developers community spreading the knowledge

To teach is to learn twice.

Joseph Joubert

I am a MEAN Stack entrepreneurship enthusiastic. I develop in front-end and my origin is back-end. You can feel free to contact me if you are in need or just want anything, I will become more than happy to help ;)
