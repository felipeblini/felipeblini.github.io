About Me

Hi, I'm Felipe from Brazil, I'm a Full-Stack Web Developer with 12 years of experience and I'm also an entrepreneur, I've been working on various projects, from small and innovative startups to legacies softwares in multinational companies and in Brazil government. I have experience as a Senior Developer abroad, in Germany, Spain, Hungary, Australia and I was co-founder of an awarded startup at DAL (Desarollando America Latina) that won an acceleration in Chile as a premium.

I've started my career as a PHP and MySql Developer, I went through many years developing in .NET Framework, C# and Sql Server, I've worked with Java, Hibernate, Mysql, Spring and Maven for a year when I was abroad and now I've focused all my time on learning more and develop with JavaScript that was always my favourite language that I learned at the university 12 years ago when I developed my first app.

I've created this blog intenting to enlarge my skills and to help the developers's community spreading the knowledge.

To teach is to learn twice.

Joseph Joubert

If you have any question or doubts or just want to talk, feel free to contact me any time, I will become more than happy to help and exchange knowledge and experiences ;)
